INCUBATION PROGRAM FOR SUSTAINABLE VALUE

An initiative for technological innovations and solutions that can create and enhance sustainability impact across sectors and businesses.

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Sustainability Value Incubation Program aims to support startups with technology solutions that can help businesses to identify & analyze risks, patterns, and opportunities, or to deliver & measure efficiency & efficacy of products, systems, operations, decisions to achieve and enhance sustainability.

FOCUS AREAS

Sustainable Value Chains

Technologies that provide closed loop systems to minimize or reduce the resource wastages and enable longer usage of products, equipment, infrastructure, and other available resources.

Transition to Net Zero

Technological solutions that combine designs, methods, business tools towards staying net positive and improving better production and usage of energy.

Sustainable Technology

Solutions that use cloud technologies efficiently and support responsible migration to the digital ecosystem, with a proactive approach to minimize minimal carbon footprint, enhance energy saving and make energy consumption more efficient.

Sustainability Measurement & Analytics

Solutions that help in diagnosing & defining operation and financial risks of decisions and subsequently better measure & analyze its ESG impacts on the business and the environment.

OFFERINGS

Seed Fund support of INR 10 Lacs

Customised and one-on-one business advisory support

Access to experts, partners, & early adopters

Startup Kit worth 60000 USD

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Applicants need to match the following:

  • You have a startup entity registered and based in India.

  • You are working in any of the mentioned focus areas.

  • Your innovative product / solution is ready for commercialization.

ABOUT US

Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) RAISE is India's 1st startup incubation Centre focusing on United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by delivering solutions through start-ups to environmental problems. We work on nurturing and supporting start-ups that use technology and creativity to solve social and environmental problems. We support mission-driven founders and start-ups by providing all necessary support to achieve their mission. We are part of the prestigious Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) and NITI Aayog, Government of India.

We concentrate on sustainability related start-ups. AIC RAISE is a platform for bringing brilliant ideas to life in the business sector. We look for the benefit of society and the environment, and we are to develop ideas that appear to be economically viable while also providing solutions to global issues.

We connect experts from various industries to startups, to guide our startups and we are for new ideas and start-ups that can lead to long-term solutions to India's social and economic issues. We have incubated 65+ startups in our incubation centre till now. We also conduct events such as SDG gamification, Hackolics, TechStars StartUp weekend to promote awareness among students about SDGs and its impotance in future.

FAQ

1. How will the startups benefit from the program ?

We will support the selected startups with seed funds upto INR 10 lakhs, network connections, access to experts, business advisory and one-on-one strategic discussions to build and scale up the respective ventures.

2. How will I know whether my application has been selected ?

If your application gets shortlisted for the subsequent rounds, we will send you an email confirming this.

3. Can anyone from the team register or apply for the program ?

We encourage the founder or any of the co-founders to register when applying for the program. Firstly, the founder’s perspective helps us to understand the business better. Secondly, it saves time from getting appropriate approvals from the founder(s) in subsequent stages and lowering the chances of disqualification.

4. On what parameters are you going to evaluate my application ?

We are looking for startups with a strong founding team, unique and innovative solution, alignment to the sector, and potential of the business to scale.
